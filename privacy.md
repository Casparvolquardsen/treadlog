# Privacy Policy for TreadLog

**Last updated: March 20, 2026**

## Overview

TreadLog is a running shoe tracker app for iPhone. This privacy policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

## Data We Access

### HealthKit Data

TreadLog requests read-only access to the following HealthKit data:

- **Workouts** — type, distance, duration, and date of running workouts
- **Route data** — GPS coordinates associated with workouts, used to display route maps

This access is used solely to display your workout history and calculate mileage accumulated on each of your shoes.

## How Your Data Is Used

- All data processing happens **entirely on your device**.
- HealthKit data is **never transmitted off your device**.
- HealthKit data is **never shared with third parties**.
- HealthKit data is **never used for advertising or analytics**.

## Data Storage

Shoe and workout assignment data you enter in TreadLog is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all app data by deleting TreadLog from your device. You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in the iOS Settings app under **Privacy & Security > Health > TreadLog**.

## Children's Privacy

TreadLog is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.
